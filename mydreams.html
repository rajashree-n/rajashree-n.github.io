After a long time!

Umm.. Hello after a very long break I decided to write a new blog. I know it was too long break.. haha.. never-mind, My exams are starting in 2 days, I'm not at all tensed about it. Well recently I had an horrible experience. Umm.. Yes, everybody get dreams. Dreams are just the depiction or imagination of all the thoughts in your mind. Dreams can be beautiful, horrifying and just a simple one. But I get lil weird dreams. Yes, too weird. And my past two dream did come true. I know you won't believe me, but they really did come true. Even I was horrified and felt like I had Dejavu. I suddenly started feeling that I had superpowers.. hehe I'm the new Super Woman in the town!! I thought that my dreams are true in real world but this not the case. Well those dreams were actually very different and didn't ever thought this would happen but actually the dreams we get are nothing but our thoughts. That day when I study on particular topic a lot or spend a lot time with someone that person comes in my dream or that topic comes in my dream as a person.. lol yes.. hehe this actually happened when I was studying a lot about JavaScript recently, I got dream where I was hanging out with JS.. I know I'm mad and I get stupid dreams. But what to do now I have no control over my dreams. Also few days back when I had to mug up bunch of words for GRE I did get those words in my dream. Its crazy, I know. So, dreams are nothing but your thoughts. But sometime you get dreams that aren't linked to your thoughts. Well this is because you did think about the thing in your mind before at least for a second and didn't paid much attention on it. But still somewhere or the other that thing might come in your dream.

It is said that the person who gets calm sleep is the most happiest one. May be it is true. But I dream everyday, so I never get to sleep calmly this doesn't mean that I'm not happy but it means my mind is adventurous hehe.. yes it doesn't take rest for a day even.

This is me and my dream. In the start I said that Dreams are beautiful but now I would say that “Dreams are advantureous!“
