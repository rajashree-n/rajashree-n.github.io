Mumbai University BE Study Hacks!

Saturday 17 December 2016
favorite favorites
bookmark bookmark

Hello my Engineer buddies. I hope the hack which I'm going to share will be helpful to you.

First of all let me tell you that this hack is only for those who study in the end during PL. This is specially for them :) The nerd ones can ignore this blog :p


Follow This Algorithm :-

  1. Download the MU Syllabus from internet. This is because our reference book or any other book always has extra topics in it. Now just mark the syllabus in your book accordingly and ignore extra topics.
  2. Intially start with your Assignments. Yes, Assignments cover upto 50-60% of questions in it. So, first learn assignments throughly leaving the numericals in it.
  3. Now after you are done with your assignments go for Numericals. Before starting with numericals, write down all the formulae on a paper and then start looking at solved numerical. After that try to solve atleast one numerical of each type on your own. The numerical can prove to be scoring because if your answer is correct you will get full marks but same case is not possible with theory questions.
  4. Lastly, throughly study last 4 Mumbai University paper. This is because 80% of the question are repeated. And will also give you idea about the question paper. While you study compare the question and the syllabus you have marked, because sometimes the syllabus will be revised and that question might be out of your new syllabus.

Now you are done with everything, just revise the formulae and difficult topics before exam. I'm sure you will definitely score 50+/80 in your Semester Exam.

Good Luck for your Exams!

PS: If you want to score 8+ CGPA Follow the above steps. Also see to it that you score well in your Internal assessment exam also i.e 18+/20 in all subjects. This will increase your CGPA easily.
