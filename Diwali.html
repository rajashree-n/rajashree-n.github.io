Tuesday 1 November 2016

DIWALI - The festival of light.. Umm.. Let me call it as "Festival of Love & Happiness". Yes out of all festival "Diwali" has always been in my favorite list. It is more because during this festival time I meet all my relatives and we exchange gifts with eachother. More than gifts it's the happiness of meeting everyone and sharing love is what I like the most. Moreover we get to wear new clothes and get dressup everyday, being girl it is the best part of this festival for me :D Also we decorate our house and draw Rangoli. Rangoli is my love since i love painting :p This was one of my best Rangoli till now.



All in all sharing happiness is the best part of our life. Whether we exchange gift or not but sharing love is priceless. This is why I love Diwali. And now awaiting for next Diwali.. hehe :p
